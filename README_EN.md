# UNIHIKER IO Extender
- [中文版](./README.md)<br/>

The IO Extender, specifically designed for the UNIHIKER, fully unleashes the potential of the mainboard's interfaces. It integrates 19 IO ports, 4 multifunctional sensor interfaces, 4 servo drivers, and dedicated ports for AI cameras. Featuring dual Type-C and MicroUSB power supply ports with anti-misinsertion design, it seamlessly integrates with the micro:bit ecosystem, providing a highly stable expansion solution for STEAM education.
